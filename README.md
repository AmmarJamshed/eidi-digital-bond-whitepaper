# Eidi Digital Bond Program — White Paper

Public policy white paper on the **Eidi Digital Bond Program (EDBP)**: a proposal to replace cash Eidi with government-backed digital bonds in Pakistan, integrated with RAAST and distributed through banks and fintech wallets.

## Live site

https://ammarjamshed.github.io/eidi-digital-bond-whitepaper/

**Version:** 1.1 (July 2026)

## Contents

- Upfront problem statement & behavioural economics of Eid cash
- Diaspora / overseas Pakistani Eidi use case
- Expanded stakeholder value propositions
- Data Sources & Methodology annex
